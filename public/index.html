Why Hair Authority Directory?

Hair Authority Directory™ was created to give men and women suffering from hair loss an information tool they can use with confidence to find the best qualified professionals and products. It is the most comprehensive, unbiased and user-friendly directory on the internet. It is managed by a team of hair loss professionals with over forty years of experience.

Information You Can Trust

This one-of-a-kind directory is 100% objective. It is not affiliated with any commercial or special interest group. It does not accept advertising and it does not promote or endorse any product or service. It allows you to compare alternate suppliers and make a rational decision about what is best for you. This is the only directory you will ever need.

How We Are Different

Hair Authority Directory was built on the foundations of The International Hair Authority, a professional publication founded in 1997 as a management resource for salon and medical clinic owners and managers. The Authority has now extended coverage to include men and women seeking top-level information they can trust. There is no better way of communicating with people seeking serious and trustworthy information in order to make better hairloss decisions.

100% Independent

Unlike other special-interest groups and deep-pocket advertisers, Hair Authority Directory is not beholden to any interest or commercial group. It lists every professional, flags special skills, professional affiliations, specialized training, and awards so you can find the best of the best.
