Why Hair Authority Directory?

Hair Authority Directory™ was created to give men and women suffering from hair loss an information tool they can use with confidence to find the best qualified professionals and products.

It is the most comprehensive, unbiased and user-friendly directory on the internet. It is managed by a team of hair loss professionals with over forty years of experience.

Information You Can Trust

This one-of-a-kind directory is 100% objective. It is not affiliated with any commercial or special interest group. It does not accept advertising and it does not promote or endorse any product or service.

It allows you to compare alternate suppliers and make a rational decision about what is best for you. This is the only directory you will ever need.

How We Are Different

Unlike other special-interest groups and deep-pocket advertisers, the Hair Authority Directory is not beholden to any interest or commercial group.
